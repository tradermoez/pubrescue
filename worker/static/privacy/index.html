Privacy Policy

Last updated June 2026

PubRescue ("we", "us") operates the file-conversion service at pubrescue.onrender.com and related tools. This policy explains what we collect and how we handle it. We keep it short because our approach is simple: we collect as little as possible.

Files you upload

  • Files you upload for conversion are processed on our server solely to perform the conversion you requested.
  • They are never shared, sold, or used for any other purpose.
  • They are automatically deleted within 24 hours. We do not retain copies.

Payments

Payments are processed by PayPal. We do not see or store your card details. We receive only the confirmation that a payment succeeded, so we can unlock your download.

Information we collect

  • Basic, standard web server logs (e.g. IP address, browser type) used to operate and secure the service.
  • If you contact us by email, we keep that correspondence to reply to you.
  • We do not sell personal information.

Pinterest and social integrations

We operate our own social media accounts (including Pinterest) to share our products. When we use the Pinterest API, it is to publish and manage content on our own accounts and to read analytics for our own posts. This does not access your personal social media accounts or data in any way.

Cookies

We use only the minimal cookies/local state required for the conversion flow to function (for example, to remember your current job). We do not use advertising trackers.

Your choices

To request deletion of any correspondence or ask a privacy question, email support@pubrescue.app.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the date above.
